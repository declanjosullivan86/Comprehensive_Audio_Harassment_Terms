## 1. Executive Summary of Changes
The inclusion of **"Safety/Lethality Scores (1-10)"** and **"Medical/Psychological Dangers"** has fundamentally transformed the nature of the project.

It has evolved from a **Descriptive Glossary** (defining what things *are*) into a **Risk Assessment Tool** (defining what things *do* to a human). This shift creates a functional utility for victims and legal professionals that does not currently exist in the public domain.
